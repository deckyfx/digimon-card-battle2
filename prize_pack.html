Prize Pack List

Data on this section was contributed by MXCM. The original board is available here:
https://gamefaqs.gamespot.com/boards/526754-digimon-digital-card-battle/79090940

A few general notes about Prize Packs:

  • Digi-Part 126 [Rare Card might appear after battle] and 127 [Rare Card even more likely to appear] add some cards to each list, and always appear in the first position of a pack.
    For example, if you wanted to get 253 Cherrymon's Mist from the Great Pack, apparently there would be a better chance of getting it if you don't have digi-part 126 and 127 equipped.
  • For emulator players:
    • The last chance to get Blue Packs with a 100% chance is before beating Mimi for the first time. You get it from Garurumon from Igloo City Battle Arena.
    • The last chance to get Yellow Packs with a 100% chance is before completing the Jungle City Extra Arena with Halsemon / Shurimon / Sylphymon as the opponents. You get it from Submarimon in Igloo City Extra Arena with Ankylomon / Digmon / Submarimon as the opponents.
    • The last chance to get Option Packs with 100% chance is before BK WarGreymon appears in the Beginner City and permanently updates the arena. You get it from WarGreymon in the Beginner City Battle Arena with Rosemon / Greymon / WarGreymon as the opponents.

The "+" mark denotes a rare card, which is available only if digi-part 126 and/or 127 are equipped.
